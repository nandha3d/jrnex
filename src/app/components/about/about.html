Our Story

From a Prototype
to a Powerhouse

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Tamil Nadu, JRNEX began with a bold conviction: drones are more than cameras with wings — they're the nervous system of tomorrow's industries.

What started as a small team experimenting with UAV prototypes has grown into a full-spectrum technology company. We design proprietary hardware, develop intelligent software, and deploy end-to-end autonomous solutions across agriculture, mining, mapping, and industrial inspection.

Today, our platforms process terabytes of aerial data daily, turning raw imagery into actionable intelligence that drives efficiency, safety, and sustainable growth for our clients.
