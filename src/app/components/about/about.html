Our Journey

From a Prototype to a Powerhouse

Founded in 2020, JRNEX started with a simple belief: that drones could do more than just capture photos. We saw the potential for aerial data to solve complex industrial problems—from detecting crop diseases months before they become visible to the naked eye, to mapping thousands of acres of rugged mining terrain in hours.

Today, we are a full-spectrum technology company, designing our own hardware and software to provide end-to-end solutions for the most demanding industries.
