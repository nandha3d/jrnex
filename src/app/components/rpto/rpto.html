Our Journey

Why We Launched RPTO Training

With India's drone industry rapidly expanding, skilled pilots are in high demand across sectors like agriculture, surveying, defense, logistics, and disaster management. Our deep experience in drone technology made us realize that a lack of trained professionals was a major challenge in the industry.

We launched our DGCA-approved RPTO to bridge this gap, offering world-class training, hands-on flying experience, and AI-powered simulations to equip pilots with the skills needed for safe and efficient drone operations.
